About

Bon Appetit restaurant is a restaurant located in Melbourne Australia serving French cuisines in the past 10 years. It was such a honour that we achieved the Michelin 3 stars award.

Bon Appetit restaurant is specialized in the traditional French cuisines, the menu is designed by their celebrity chefs. From the entrée to the desserts, are made with the chef’s secret recipe. The red and white wines are imported from France, we purchases these wines from the vineyard that achieves the Wine Award in France directly. People will not be diappointed with the food and beverage of Bon Appetit restaurant.

Bon Appetit restaurant is managed by a team of hospitality professionals. We are grad to have Mr. Michael Karl to be the head chef and to in-charge of the kitchen. A experienced restaurant manager and the head waiter are leading all the servers in the restaurant. They ensure the food and service providing to the customers are on point.

Banquet and event

Bon Appetit restaurant has a grand interior design and a big outdoor sitting area, which make it suitable to host banquets, weddings, and parties. The indoor sitting area can seat up to 280 people. The outdoor area can contain up to 350 people.


We have a lot of customers chose to host their parties or wedding in Bon Appetit restaurant, we are the expert of banqueting! We welcome the customer to enquire about booking the venue, please call +61 36257554 to enquire or make a booking.
